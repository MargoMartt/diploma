Shopping Questions

How long does delivery take?

Generally stock items are despatched within 1 to 14 days depending on any cutting requirements. Some product pages may specify additional lead times. For all orders - including non-stock items - we will confirm exact lead time when we send you our official order acknowledgement. If you have a particularly urgent requirement, please contact us so that we can ensure we can meet with your expectation.

How can I pay for my order?

All internet orders must be paid for when you place your order. We accept payment by credit or debit card. All card transactions are processed through our 100% secure payment provider PayPal. For other payment options eg bank transfer, cheque, please contact us by telephone to process your order. Existing customers can apply for a credit account which can also be used to make online purchases.

I've sent an email enquiry, when can I expect a reply?

TDuring normal working hours Monday to Friday - we aim to reply to all emails within 12 hours. If you need an urgent response, please Live Chat with us, or call us.

Where can you deliver to?

We use our own transport, together with preferred carriers to ensure we can deliver to every part of the Belarus. Our website can only process orders being delivered to Belarus addresses - for all non-Belarus deliveries please contact us by telephone to process your order.

Different Questions

I'm heaving problems ordering online?

On rare occasions, some customers may experience problems ordering online or navigating around our site. We're really sorry if you are having difficulty - please contact the sales office to discuss your requirements.

How do I know if my order has been successful?

Once your order has been completed and you confirm, you will see info in your account. This will contain your order number and full details of your order. We suggest you create an account when you order so that you can easily review previous orders, enjoy speedy checkout and save favourite pieces to your wish list.

Where can I find previous season items?

If a product is online, but showing as out of stock, our customer service team may be able to help advise on store stock or restocking information. If an item is not showing on the website, we are unable to provide further information.

Can I order as a gift?

Yes, simply choose address and write ‘deliver gift’ in your cart, then when you are taken to the checkout page you will have a further option add info & write 'this is a gift’ – please tick yes and you will be able to add a gift message and prices will be removed from the invoice.

Miscellaneous Questions

I want to receive an order and the invoice at a different address. Is this possible?

This is not a problem at all. You can enter a different delivery address, for example, if you want to send a gift or you need the items at a different address than the invoice address. When you opt for this, we will send the package to the delivery address, and the invoice will be sent to the invoice address.

Why have I only received part of my order?

Most of our online orders are picked and shipped from our warehouse in Borisov, however, certain products are only available in our stores. Therefore, you may receive separate deliveries. Rest assured that our standard delivery timeframes still apply and there are no extra charges for additional shipments.

Why has my order been cancelled?

On rare occasions, we may need to cancel your order. In this instance, you'll receive an email to notify you as soon as possible, and you will be refunded fully for cancelled items.

Do I have to create an account to order?

You are required to create an account. We do recommend that you do. When you register, you can easily look back at your order history, and with a repeat order, you don't have to enter your information again.
